Robotic Arm – Gen-1

Reverse-engineered, modeled, prototyped, and validated a 4-DOF robotic arm. Built CAD in Siemens NX, produced prototypes through FDM 3D printing and laser cutting, designed control electronics in KiCad, and programmed motion control on Arduino and PlatformIO.

This project strengthened my skills in mechanical design, electronics integration, prototyping, and motion control while helping me understand the full process from concept to functional validation.
